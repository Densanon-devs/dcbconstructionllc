Our Story

Remodeling Homes, Building Relationships

DCB Construction LLC was founded in 2015 by David C. Busse with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a remodeling experience they can actually enjoy. What started as a passion for transforming outdated spaces into functional, beautiful rooms has grown into one of Northern Idaho's most trusted contracting companies.

From the very beginning, we set out to do things differently. We answer our phones, show up on time, and treat every home like it's our own. Our team specializes in kitchen remodeling, bathroom remodeling, tile and marble installation, flooring, walk-in showers, in-floor heating, and trim and finishing work — along with basement remodels, whole-home renovations, and additions.

We proudly serve homeowners across Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Hayden, Sandpoint, and the wider Kootenai, Bonner, Boundary, Benewah, and Shoshone County areas. Whether you're updating a single bathroom or gutting an entire kitchen, we bring the same level of care and attention to detail to every project.

The DCB Difference

Why Homeowners Choose Us

There's no shortage of contractors in Northern Idaho, but finding one you can actually trust is another story. Here's what sets DCB Construction apart:

  • Licensed & Insured — Idaho General Contractor Registration RCE-52572, fully active. We carry full liability coverage, so your home and investment are always protected.
  • Local to Northern Idaho — We live and work in the communities we serve. Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Hayden, and Sandpoint are our neighbors.
  • No Subcontractor Runaround — You deal directly with us. We don't hand your project off to strangers and disappear.
  • Detailed Timelines — Before we start, you'll receive a clear project schedule so you can plan around the work with confidence.
  • Material Guidance — Choosing tile, flooring, or fixtures can be overwhelming. We help you navigate options that fit your style and budget.
  • Permit Handling — When permits are required, we take care of the paperwork and inspections so you don't have to.
