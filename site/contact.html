Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from homeowners.

Getting a free estimate is easy. You can call us directly at (425) 737-0645, email us at Dcbconstructionllccda@gmail.com, or fill out the contact form above. We'll schedule a convenient time to visit your home, discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide a detailed, no-obligation estimate — typically within a few business days.

We proudly serve homeowners throughout Northern Idaho, including Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Hayden, Athol, and Sandpoint. If you're in the surrounding area and aren't sure if we cover your location, give us a call — we're happy to discuss your project.

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A straightforward bathroom update might take 1-2 weeks, while a full kitchen remodel typically runs 4-8 weeks. During your estimate, we'll provide a detailed project timeline so you know exactly what to expect. We always aim to finish on schedule and keep disruptions to a minimum.

Yes. When your project requires permits — such as for structural changes, plumbing, or electrical work — we handle all the paperwork, submissions, and inspections on your behalf. You won't need to visit any offices or deal with the process yourself. We make sure everything is done to code and properly documented.

We understand that a remodel is a significant investment. While we don't offer in-house financing, we can work with you on flexible payment schedules and phased project plans to fit your budget. We also provide detailed estimates upfront so you can explore financing options with your bank or credit union before work begins.
