Kitchen Remodeling in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho

Custom kitchen remodels for Coeur d'Alene homeowners — from condo updates downtown to full renovations on the lakeshore.

Coeur d'Alene homes range from historic cottages near downtown to modern lakefront properties on the water. The one thing they have in common: kitchens that were built for a different era. Closed-off galley layouts, dated cabinetry, and counters that have seen better decades are the norm in CDA homes built before the early 2000s.

DCB Construction LLC specializes in opening up those layouts — removing the wall between the kitchen and living area, adding islands that earn their keep, and replacing tired cabinetry with modern shaker, slab-front, or two-tone designs. We work with homeowners across Coeur d'Alene to design kitchens that match how families actually live and entertain today.

Serving Coeur d'Alene Homeowners

Coeur d'Alene's housing stock is a mix of historic homes built in the 1920s through 1960s, mid-century ranches, and newer construction along the lakefront and in master-planned communities like Riverstone. Each era brings its own kitchen quirks — older homes have small footprints with awkward flow, while newer homes often have builder-grade kitchens that homeowners want to upgrade with custom finishes.

We've worked on kitchens across CDA neighborhoods including downtown, Fernan Lake Village, Sanders Beach, and the Hayden Lake side. Whatever your home's vintage, we plan around its character — preserving what makes the space special while modernizing what doesn't.
