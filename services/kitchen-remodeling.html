Kitchen Remodeling in Northern Idaho

The kitchen is the heart of every home — where meals are shared, memories are made, and daily life happens. At DCB Construction LLC, we specialize in transforming outdated kitchens into beautiful, functional spaces that reflect how your family actually lives. Whether you are dreaming of a complete layout overhaul or a targeted refresh with new countertops and cabinetry, our team delivers results that exceed expectations.

Our Kitchen Remodeling Services

We offer a comprehensive range of kitchen remodeling services to handle every aspect of your project under one roof:

  • Custom cabinetry & cabinet refacing
  • Countertop installation (granite, quartz, marble)
  • Kitchen layout redesign & optimization
  • Island & peninsula installation
  • Backsplash tile work
  • Lighting & electrical upgrades
  • Plumbing fixture updates
  • Flooring replacement

Our Kitchen Remodeling Process

We follow a proven four-step process that keeps your project on track, on budget, and stress-free from start to finish.

1. In-Home Consultation

We visit your home, listen to your goals, take measurements, and assess your existing kitchen. You will receive a detailed, no-obligation estimate so you can make an informed decision with full confidence.

2. Design & Material Selection

Together we finalize the layout, choose materials — from cabinetry finishes to countertop slabs — and lock in every detail before work begins. Our team helps you navigate options to stay within your budget without sacrificing quality.

3. Expert Construction

Our skilled crew handles demolition, framing, plumbing, electrical, and finish work with precision craftsmanship. We protect your home, keep the jobsite clean, and communicate progress every step of the way.

4. Final Walkthrough

When the project is complete, we walk you through every detail of your new kitchen. We address any final touches and make sure everything meets your expectations. We are not done until you are completely satisfied.

Why Choose DCB for Your Kitchen Remodel?

Quality Craftsmanship: Our experienced team brings years of hands-on remodeling expertise to every project. From perfectly leveled countertops to seamlessly aligned cabinetry, we deliver results that stand the test of time.

Local Expertise: As a Northern Idaho contractor, we understand the unique needs of homeowners in the Coeur d'Alene region — from seasonal considerations to local building codes and permit requirements.

Transparent Pricing: Every estimate is detailed and itemized. You will know exactly what you are paying for before any work begins — no surprises, no hidden fees, no change-order games.

Kitchen Remodeling FAQs

Most kitchen remodels take between 4 and 8 weeks depending on the scope of the project. A simple refresh with new countertops and cabinet refacing may take closer to 3 weeks, while a full gut renovation with layout changes typically runs 6 to 8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase so you know exactly what to expect.

Kitchen remodel costs vary widely based on the size of the space, materials selected, and scope of work. A mid-range kitchen remodel in Northern Idaho typically ranges from $25,000 to $60,000, while higher-end renovations with custom cabinetry and premium materials can exceed that. We provide free, detailed estimates so you can plan your budget with confidence.
