Bathroom Remodeling in Northern Idaho

Your bathroom should be more than just functional — it should be a space you enjoy spending time in. At DCB Construction LLC, we transform outdated bathrooms into beautiful, modern retreats that combine smart design with lasting quality. Whether you need a full gut renovation or a targeted upgrade, our team handles every detail from demolition to the final grout line with precision and care.

Our Bathroom Remodeling Services

We provide end-to-end bathroom remodeling services, managing every trade and detail so you have a single point of contact throughout your project:

  • Complete bathroom gut renovations
  • Shower & tub replacement
  • Vanity & cabinet installation
  • Tile work (floors, walls, showers)
  • Plumbing fixture upgrades
  • Lighting & ventilation
  • Accessibility modifications (grab bars, walk-in showers)
  • Storage solutions

From Outdated to Outstanding

A bathroom remodel is one of the most impactful improvements you can make to your home. Cracked tile, leaky fixtures, poor lighting, and cramped layouts do not just look dated — they affect how your home feels every single day. Our renovation process addresses both form and function, giving you a space that works better, looks stunning, and adds real value to your home.

We work with a wide range of materials and styles, from clean contemporary designs with large-format porcelain tile to warm, classic looks with natural stone and wood-tone vanities. Whatever your vision, we bring the craftsmanship to make it happen.

Why DCB for Your Bathroom?

Specialized Expertise: Bathrooms require careful coordination of waterproofing, plumbing, electrical, and tile work. Our team has years of experience handling the technical complexity behind beautiful bathroom finishes — so nothing is overlooked and everything is built to last.

Attention to Detail: From perfectly aligned tile patterns and flush grout lines to properly sealed shower enclosures, we obsess over the details that separate average work from exceptional results. Every joint, every edge, every transition gets the care it deserves.

Transparent Process: You receive a detailed, itemized estimate before work begins. We communicate progress throughout the build and keep you informed of any decisions that come up along the way. No surprises, no hidden costs — just honest, quality work.

Bathroom Remodeling FAQs

A typical bathroom remodel takes between 2 and 5 weeks depending on the scope of work. A straightforward update with new fixtures, vanity, and paint may be completed in about 2 weeks, while a full gut renovation with custom tile work, plumbing relocation, and layout changes can take 4 to 5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the planning phase.

Our bathroom remodels can include everything from demolition and framing to plumbing, electrical, tile installation, vanity and cabinet work, fixture upgrades, lighting, ventilation, and final paint or trim. The scope depends entirely on your needs and budget — we can handle a targeted upgrade or a complete transformation.
