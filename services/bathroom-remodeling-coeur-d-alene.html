Bathroom Remodeling in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho

Custom bathroom remodels in Coeur d'Alene — from compact downtown bathrooms to spa-style master suites in lakefront homes.

Bathrooms are where Coeur d'Alene's housing stock shows its age the most. Older CDA homes were built when bathrooms were treated as utility spaces — small, dim, with awkward layouts. Even mid-century homes typically have undersized master baths and tub-shower combos that no longer fit how people want to use them.

DCB Construction LLC has remodeled bathrooms across Coeur d'Alene from small condo retrofits to full master suite gut renovations. We specialize in walk-in and curbless showers, custom tile work, and accessibility upgrades that work for aging-in-place. Whatever the size of your CDA bathroom, we design around it rather than fighting against it.

Serving Coeur d'Alene Homeowners

Coeur d'Alene homeowners increasingly want master bathrooms that feel like spa retreats, not utility rooms. That means freestanding tubs, walk-in showers, double vanities, and tile work that elevates the entire space. Aging-in-place upgrades are also in high demand — curbless showers, grab bars integrated into tile design, and comfort-height fixtures that don't compromise on style.

We've worked on bathrooms in CDA homes ranging from 1920s downtown bungalows to brand-new lakefront builds. Each one is approached with the same priorities: thorough demolition and inspection, professional waterproofing, and tile work that holds up for decades.
