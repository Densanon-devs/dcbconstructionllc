Professional Tile Installation Services

Expert tile work for every room in your home, from floors and backsplashes to showers and fireplaces.

Tile is one of the most versatile and durable materials in home remodeling. It brings beauty, functionality, and lasting value to virtually any surface in your home. From kitchen backsplashes and bathroom floors to fireplace surrounds and entryway accents, professionally installed tile transforms ordinary spaces into something exceptional.

At DCB Construction LLC, tile installation is at the heart of what we do. Our team brings years of hands-on experience to every project, delivering precise layouts, clean cuts, and flawless grout lines that elevate the finished result. Whether you are choosing classic ceramic subway tile or an intricate natural stone mosaic, we have the skill and attention to detail to bring your vision to life.

Tile Installation Services

We handle tile installation for a wide range of residential applications. No matter the room or surface, our process ensures a durable, watertight, and visually stunning result:

  • Bathroom floor and wall tile — Complete bathroom tile work including floors, shower walls, tub surrounds, and accent features. We install proper waterproofing membranes to protect against moisture damage.
  • Kitchen backsplash tile — Backsplashes that protect your walls and add character to your kitchen. From simple subway patterns to elaborate mosaic designs, we match your kitchen style perfectly.
  • Shower and tub surrounds — Fully tiled shower enclosures and tub surrounds with built-in niches, shelves, and decorative accents. Every installation includes industry-standard waterproofing.
  • Fireplace surrounds and hearths — Custom tile work that frames your fireplace and creates a stunning focal point for your living space. We work with heat-rated materials designed for safe use around fireplaces.
  • Entryway and mudroom tile — Durable, easy-to-clean tile floors for high-traffic entry areas. We select materials that stand up to Northern Idaho weather and daily wear.
  • Outdoor tile work — Porcelain and natural stone tile for covered patios, outdoor kitchens, and other exterior applications using frost-resistant materials rated for our climate.

Tile Materials We Work With

Choosing the right tile material is essential to achieving the look, durability, and maintenance level you want. We source quality products from trusted suppliers and help you select the best option for each application:

  • Ceramic tile — An affordable, versatile option available in a huge range of colors, sizes, and finishes. Ideal for walls, backsplashes, and light-traffic floors.
  • Porcelain tile — Denser and more durable than ceramic, porcelain is excellent for floors, showers, and outdoor applications. Available in wood-look, stone-look, and contemporary finishes.
  • Natural stone (marble, travertine, slate) — One-of-a-kind beauty with natural variation in color and texture. Each piece is unique, creating a rich, organic look that cannot be replicated.
  • Glass mosaic tile — Luminous, light-catching tiles perfect for shower accents, backsplash features, and decorative borders that add depth and visual interest.
  • Subway tile — A timeless classic that works in virtually any setting. Available in standard ceramic, beveled, handmade, and colored variations for different aesthetic effects.
  • Large-format tiles — Tiles larger than 12 by 24 inches that create a seamless, modern appearance with fewer grout lines. Requires expert installation for a flat, even result.
  • Decorative and patterned tile — Encaustic, Moroccan, geometric, and hand-painted tiles that serve as statement pieces in kitchens, bathrooms, and entryways.

Our Tile Installation Process

Quality tile installation is as much about what happens beneath the surface as what you see on top. Our proven process ensures every project is built on a solid foundation:

  • Surface preparation — We evaluate and prepare the substrate, ensuring it is level, clean, and structurally sound. For wet areas, we apply waterproofing membranes and vapor barriers to prevent moisture damage behind the tile.
  • Layout and pattern planning — Before any tile is set, we dry-lay the pattern to confirm alignment, symmetry, and cut placement. This step prevents awkward sliver cuts at edges and ensures a balanced, intentional design.
  • Precision cutting — We use professional-grade wet saws and tile cutters to make clean, accurate cuts around outlets, fixtures, corners, and transitions. Precise cutting is what separates amateur tile work from professional results.
  • Expert installation — Each tile is set with the correct thinset mortar, proper back-buttering technique, and consistent spacing. We check for level and lippage throughout the installation to deliver a smooth, even surface.
  • Grouting and sealing — We apply grout carefully, cleaning as we go to prevent haze and residue. Natural stone and porous tiles receive sealer to protect against staining and moisture absorption, extending the life of your investment.

Why Professional Tile Installation Matters

Tile installation may look straightforward, but the difference between a DIY job and professional work becomes obvious over time. Improperly installed tile can lead to cracked grout, loose tiles, water intrusion behind walls, and costly damage that is far more expensive to fix than it would have been to do right the first time.

Waterproofing is especially critical in showers, tub surrounds, and bathroom floors. Without a properly installed membrane system, moisture seeps through grout and into the wall or subfloor, causing mold, rot, and structural damage that remains hidden until the problem is severe. Our installations follow manufacturer specifications and industry best practices to ensure your tile is protected from day one.

Beyond durability, professional installation delivers the visual precision that makes tile work truly shine. Symmetrical layouts, consistent spacing, clean transitions between materials, and perfectly aligned patterns are the hallmarks of expert craftsmanship — and the details that make your home feel finished and polished.

Tile Installation Questions

Tile installation costs typically range from $6 to $20 per square foot for labor, depending on the complexity of the project, tile size, and pattern. Material costs are additional and vary widely based on the type of tile selected. Simple floor installations with standard porcelain tend toward the lower end, while intricate mosaic patterns, natural stone, and shower installations with waterproofing fall on the higher end. We provide detailed, itemized estimates after assessing your specific project.

Timeline depends on the scope of work. A kitchen backsplash typically takes 1 to 2 days. A bathroom floor may take 2 to 3 days. A full shower tile installation, including waterproofing, can take 5 to 7 days. These estimates include surface preparation, installation, grouting, and curing time. We provide a clear schedule before work begins and keep you informed throughout the process.
