Post Falls — Spokane River & Community

Trusted Remodeling for a Growing Community

Post Falls has transformed over the past two decades from a quiet mill town along the Spokane River into one of the most desirable family communities in the Idaho panhandle. With its proximity to both Coeur d'Alene and the Spokane metro area, Post Falls offers homeowners the best of both worlds — small-town living with big-city access.

That growth has brought a wide mix of housing stock. Established neighborhoods near Falls Park and the Spokane River corridor feature mature homes from the 1970s through 1990s that are prime candidates for kitchen and bathroom updates. Meanwhile, newer developments in areas like Riverview Estates, Prairie Falls, and along Seltice Way have homes that, while more recent, often benefit from upgraded finishes, custom tile work, or the addition of in-floor heating for those cold Northern Idaho mornings.

DCB Construction has been serving Post Falls homeowners throughout this period of growth. We understand the construction styles common in each era of development here, from the builder-grade finishes in early 2000s subdivisions to the solid-but-dated layouts in the older neighborhoods near Treaty Rock and Q'emiln Park. That hands-on experience allows us to recommend the right approach for your specific home and budget.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Post Falls

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Post Falls?

Kitchen remodel costs in Post Falls vary widely based on scope, materials, and layout changes. A moderate kitchen renovation with new countertops, cabinetry, backsplash, and flooring typically falls in the mid-range for our area. We provide detailed, itemized estimates during a free in-home consultation so you know exactly what to expect before committing.

Do you work in the newer Post Falls subdivisions?

Yes. We work throughout Post Falls, including newer developments like Prairie Falls, Riverview Estates, and the neighborhoods along Pleasant View Road. Many of these homes were built with standard finishes that homeowners want to upgrade within a few years of purchase. We specialize in those kinds of targeted improvements — replacing builder-grade materials with custom, high-quality finishes.

Can you help increase my Post Falls home's resale value?

Absolutely. Kitchen and bathroom remodels consistently rank among the highest-return home improvements. In the competitive Post Falls real estate market, updated kitchens, modern bathrooms, and quality flooring can make a significant difference when it comes time to sell. We can help you prioritize upgrades that maximize your return on investment.

How far in advance should I schedule my remodel?

We recommend reaching out at least four to six weeks before your desired start date, especially during the busy spring and summer months. This gives us time for a consultation, design finalization, material ordering, and scheduling. For larger projects, more lead time is helpful. Contact us anytime for a free estimate and we will work with your timeline.
